Tracking Rising World Temperatures with Extreme Weather Events

This page will show several visualizations that demonstrate that increase in global temperatures correlate with an increase in extreme weather events. Below you can find information about worldwide reports of hurricanes, floods, drought, and wilfires worldwide over the past century. You can see clearly that as global temperatures have risen, so have the frequency of extreme weather events.
